12:T944,<h1>Proprietary Internal Product Development</h1>
<p>A highly successful, proprietary business solution designed, architected, and built from the ground up to streamline operations and generate new revenue streams.</p>
<h2>Technical Showcase</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Role</strong>: Lead Architect &amp; Developer (End-to-End Delivery)</li>
<li><strong>Architecture</strong>: Ruby Web API &amp; Services</li>
<li><strong>Backend &amp; Infrastructure</strong>: Ruby, Docker, SQL, Azure MSSQL</li>
<li><strong>Hosting &amp; CI/CD</strong>: Azure DevOps pipelines &amp; cloud hosting environment</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h2>1. The Challenge</h2>
<p>The business identified a key operational bottleneck and revenue opportunity that off-the-shelf software could not address. The objective was to plan, design, and implement a custom internal product to:</p>
<ol>
<li>Handle complex workflows and state transitions.</li>
<li>Integrate securely with core legacy business databases and third-party services.</li>
<li>Scale efficiently without exponential infrastructure costs.</li>
<li>Support offline-first or low-connectivity client states.</li>
</ol>
<hr>
<h2>2. Technical Implementation &amp; Design decisions</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>API Architecture</strong>: Designed a clean, RESTful Ruby-based API with separation of concerns and robust routing models.</li>
<li><strong>Database Tuning</strong>: Designed database schemas with Azure MSSQL, utilizing query optimization, tailored indices, and transactional guarantees to support high-concurrency usage.</li>
<li><strong>Containerization &amp; Environment</strong>: Built consistent development and deployment flows using Docker containers.</li>
<li><strong>CI/CD Pipeline</strong>: Configured build and release pipelines in Azure DevOps for automated integration, testing, and continuous deployment to ensure zero-downtime rolling updates.</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h2>3. Results &amp; Business Impact</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Revenue Generation</strong>: The product is actively used in production and is successfully earning significant new revenue/savings for the business.</li>
<li><strong>Adoption</strong>: 100% internal adoption within the target division, replacing multiple disjointed spreadsheet-based workflows.</li>
<li><strong>Performance</strong>: Optimized SQL query paths and state caching to keep page loads under 200ms.</li>
</ul>
